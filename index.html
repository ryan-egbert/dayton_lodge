Welcome to the Dayton Lodge

Dayton Lodge #103 has served Tippecanoe County, Indiana since the 1865. We hold our stated meetings the second Tuesday of the month at 7:00 PM. At 6:00 PM before our meetings we have an informal dinner open to visiting Masons as well as non-members who would like to know more about our order. We also host other events throughout the year, like pancake breakfasts on the second Saturday of every month, a booth at the Feast of the Hunter's Moon, and an annual Feast of Saint John the Evangelist celebrated in December.
